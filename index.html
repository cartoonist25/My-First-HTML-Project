Classic Homemade Apple Pie Recipe

A timeless American dessert that's perfect for any occasion

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • Tips

Why This Apple Pie Recipe?

Nothing beats the warm, spiced aroma of a freshly baked apple pie.
This easy-to-follow recipe guarantees a flaky, buttery crust and a perfectly sweet-tart apple filling. Perfect for holidays, family gatherings, or just because!

"The secret to perfect apple pie? A balance of tart and sweet apples, and plenty of patience when letting it cool." — Bon Appétit

Ingredients

For the Pie Crust:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter
  • 6-8 tbsp ice water

For the Apple Filling:

  • 6-7 medium apples
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp nutmeg
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter

Instructions

1. Make the Pie Crust:

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, salt, and sugar.
  2. Cut in cold butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Gradually add ice water, 1 tbsp at a time, until dough forms.
  4. Divide into two discs, wrap in plastic, and chill for 1 hour.

2. Prepare the Apple Filling:

  1. Peel, core, and slice apples into ¼-inch thick pieces.
  2. In a large bowl, toss apples with sugars, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and lemon juice.

3. Assemble the Pie:

  1. Roll out one dough disc and fit it into a 9-inch pie dish.
  2. Add the apple filling, mounding slightly in the center. Dot with butter.
  3. Roll out the second dough disc, place over filling, and crimp edges. Cut slits for venting.
  4. Brush with egg wash and sprinkle with sugar (optional).

4. Bake to Perfection:

  • Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  • Bake for 45-55 minutes, until golden and bubbly (cover edges with foil if browning too quickly).
  • Cool for 2 hours before slicing.

Tips for Success

  • Use cold butter for the flakiest crust.
  • Let pie cool to set the filling—no runny slices!
  • Serve warm with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.
  • For extra flavor, add 1/4 tsp of cardamom to the filling.
